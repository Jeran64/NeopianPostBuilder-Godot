Community Guidelines


This is where information about what is acceptable content for writers, and comic artists to post will go!

  • Users can have multiple stories concurrently
  • Submissions should be entierly the submitters own work. (Collaborations are allowed!)
  • Submitted content should take place entierly within the Neopets canon. (A.k.a. No having Naruto appear through a portal to join your story)
  • Users are responsible for submitting fully edited and formatted content. Requests for edits will not always be honored.
    • Please use compatible HTML to achieve the best quality formatting.
    • Please ensure speling and typ os are corrected.
    • Please use the best grammar you can.
    Editorial judgement will be used to reject submissions that are difficult to read, or lack clairity. If they are needed, You will be able to resubmit after changes are made.
  • Romance is allowed. Content should not be sexual, or use excessive innuendo.
  • Violence is allowed, to a certain extent. Do not use excessive violence for the sake of discomfort.
  • Plagiarism is not allowed.
  • AI Generated content is not allowed.
  • Please tag content accordingly! Below are some suggesstions for helpful kind of tags, but feel free to include other tags.

    Suggessted Kinds of Tags:
    • Content Warnings
      Examples:
      • Violence
      • Death
    • Genre
      Examples:
      • Political Mystery
      • Hurt/Comfort
      • Light Adventure and Swashbuckling
    • Pet Species Involved
      Examples:
      • Bori
      • Cybunny
      • Eyrie
    • NPCs Involved
      Examples:
      • Lord Kass
      • King Kelpbeard
      • Fyora
    • Settings Involved
      Examples:
      • Krawk Island
      • Meridell
      • The Lost Desert Plot
      • Terror Mountain
    • Themed Day, or Collab Issue
      Examples:
      • Christmas Issue
      • Beach Day Episode
    • Author Fanon Names
      Examples:
      • Castle Planners Journal
      • Henningverse
      • MCU

Editorial Guidelines


This is where the guiding philosohpies of the editorial team will go.

  • The Editorial Team will aim for for a monthly release schedule. Special themed issues, may come out in addition to these monthly issues.
  • New content will be published. Content that has previously been published elsewhere will not be prioritized unless changes have been made to made necessitating rehosting. (AKA, please do not submit your back catalog, unless you've made major revisions. Unpublished back catalog is acceptable)
  • Content that is published here does not have to be exclusivly here.
  • The editorial staff has final say over whether content makes it in, and can refuse to host content for any reason.
  • The editorial staff will not make content edits to content submitted. Some light formatting edits may occur to make content compatible with the site.
